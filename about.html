General Information

The Northside Youth Soccer League was established in 1996 to provide athletes residing in Chicago´s northside neighborhoods an evironment in which to learn and play soccer. To be a member of NYSL, yo must be between the ages of 4 - 12 and reside in Chicago northside neighborhooD.
